❝Magic is not a practice. It is a living, breathing web of energy that, with our permission, can encase our every action.❞

― Dorothy Morrison

The manipulation of air is a fickle and very strange magic. It is said that the winds are streams of mana themselves, and thus to control them, one would have to read the flow of energy around them like the waves of the ocean. Many who practise this esoteric form of magic are blind or crippled, yet have the wisdom and awareness of everyday man. Perhaps the wind speaks to them, or they have simply learned to walk with the current.

Pyromancy is the art of casting fire. Produce flame, the channel it; just as our ancestors did. A pyromancer must be in tune with nature herself. -Laurentius of the Great Swamp.

Pyromancy is the art of invoking and manipulating fire. But remember one thing. Always fear the flame, lest you be devoured by it, and lose yourself.

Dark sorceries are borne of humanity, are filled with emotion, and are drawn towards life. They are the antithesis to existence, and have been used to destroy and rip apart the balance of the realm.

Necromancy; at its core is the manipulation of life-force and the raising and commanding the dead, heaving entire graveyards to their heathenous will. An enticing magic however foul as many often find themselves succumbing to the darkness by false notions of grandeur, or of power -- to bring forth hordes of the dead, to bring back loved ones, to influence current events from the shadows, to empower ones self.

"Arcane Magic is the archetype of magic which is fueled by The Void, or more specifically mana. The Void is an empty plane of existence where things may be created with the use of ones imagination and willpower. Many teachers of the Arcane often teach students with the following proverb: "The Void is nothing, yet everything. Like our imagination, it may be dull and empty; or it can be full and colorful". The Void as a whole is simply a plane where nothing exists, unless the mage makes it exist. In invoking this pure form of magic, one may be more effective in dealing with otherwordly beings, though struggle against common plate and steel.

Through careful study of the histories of mortals and gods, the motions of the stars, and the intersections of planes, you have divined the true nature of time and are now able to bend it to your will. Chronomancers are rare wizards, and much sought after – for past, present, and future are theirs to command. Though altering the natural course of history can be dangerous, there are many who would pay great sums for the chance to eliminate threats before they materialize or to seize advantages years in advance.

Blood was thought to be life. Flesh belonged to us, the blood to deity or deities.Blood Magic is, at it’s core, the magical art of drawing power (mana) from the blood of any living being, be it yourself or any other. Use of the magic sacrifices vitality and “life” in exchange for increased power and a temporarily deeper mana pool (explained below).

It can be used to cast or enhance spells as well as cast some spells of it’s own at later stages, although it’s used most efficiently in the performance of powerful rituals that would otherwise be far too draining and taxing for even some of the most powerful Arcane mages. During the process of drawing the power from the blood, the natural energy is twisted and morphed, creating an unnatural and crude form of it’s original state.
